About us

A neighborhood bakery rooted in tradition.

We started as a small family kitchen and grew into a beloved neighborhood bakery by keeping our methods simple and our hospitality generous.

Our story

Dadin Kowa Bakery was born from a love of warm bread, buttery pastries, and the kind of welcome that turns first-time guests into regulars. Each morning, we mix, proof, and bake in small batches so every item feels fresh from the heart.

We pride ourselves on using time-tested recipes, hand-finished details, and a deeply personal service that makes every order feel special.
